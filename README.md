# Aadinath Engineering Works

Aadinath Engineering Works is a trusted engineering service provider specializing in all types of control valve repair and maintenance. The company has more than 13 years of experience in repairing and maintaining industrial valves for various sectors.

## About the Company
We specialize in maintenance and repair of different types of valves such as Sluice Valve, IBR Valve, Right Angle Valve, Non-Return Valve, Globe Valve, Ball Valve and Fabricated Valves. Our team can handle valve sizes from 2 inches to 40 inches diameter.

- Sugar Industries
- Irrigation Projects
- Distillery Sector
- Municipal Water Supply Departments
